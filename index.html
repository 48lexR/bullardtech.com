About Us

Our Services

We offer a variety of solutions for you or your business. These services include:

  • Website and Web App Development like
    • Domain registration
    • Server maintenance
    • HTTP Secure setup
    • App Development
  • Computer/Operating System Maintenance like
    • PC Building
    • PC Setup
    • Cloud-based storage setup and help

Tools We Use

For those who are curious, we use several tools to offer these services. We use Linode/Akamai cloud-based computing servers, and Cloudflare for HTTP Secure setup and monitoring. We use NameCheap for domain registration.

Behind the hood, we use AngularJS for web-apps, and Nginx to set up the back-end.

Pricing Information

We're are comitted to offering reasonable prices for our services. Our prices are negotiable (including cost upfront), but an initial price may be lower or higher depending on several factors, including

  1. The cost of the equipment required for a service
  2. The amount of time a service may take
  3. The amount of services required (for example, a single site may cost less than several sites all under one domain.)

Pricing Upfront and Finally

We require that all equipment fees be paid upfront, along with a percentage of the labor fees. This is so we're able to begin our work by buying the necessary equipment. For our website-building services, we require clients to either

  1. agree to register an account with NameCheap and/or Linode and pay server rent and record maintenance through those respective companies, or
  2. agree to reimburse Bullard Tech Consulting based on the services we continue to maintain for you.
